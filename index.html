About Me

Hey there, everyone! BabyElle here, diving into the world of writing with wide-eyed enthusiasm. As a self-proclaimed amateur, I'm navigating the labyrinth of words, guided by my love for romance-both the sweet and the steamy sides! An unapologetic Libra and an INTP-T, I'll keep my age a mystery for now (a writer's secret!). When I'm not lost in the realms of storytelling, you'll find me immersed in heartwarming K-POP melodies that fuel my creative soul. I have a strange relationship with spicy food-I can't handle it, yet I can't resist it! A freelancer by day, I wield both pen and brush as an illustrator and novelist. Join me on this journey as we explore the wonders of imagination together!
